The Quantum Archive

A comprehensive collection of fundamental quantum mechanical calculations and formulas.

01. Foundations

Planck-Einstein Relation

Energy of a photon proportional to frequency.

$$ E = h\nu = \hbar\omega $$

De Broglie Wavelength

Wave-particle duality connection.

$$ \lambda = \frac{h}{p} $$

Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle

Fundamental limit of precision for conjugate pairs.

$$ \sigma_x \sigma_p \geq \frac{\hbar}{2} $$
$$ \Delta E \Delta t \geq \frac{\hbar}{2} $$

02. Wave Equations

Time-Dependent Schrödinger Equation

The fundamental equation of motion for quantum states.

$$ i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\psi(t)\rangle = \hat{H}|\psi(t)\rangle $$

Time-Independent Schrödinger Equation

For stationary states with definite energy E.

$$ \hat{H}\psi(\mathbf{r}) = E\psi(\mathbf{r}) $$
$$ \left[ -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\nabla^2 + V(\mathbf{r}) \right]\psi(\mathbf{r}) = E\psi(\mathbf{r}) $$

Probability Current

Flow of probability density.

$$ \mathbf{j} = \frac{\hbar}{2mi} \left( \psi^* \nabla \psi - \psi \nabla \psi^* \right) $$

03. Operators

Momentum & Position Operators

In position space representation.

$$ \hat{p} = -i\hbar\nabla $$
$$ \hat{x} = x $$

Canonical Commutation Relation

Non-commutativity of position and momentum.

$$ [\hat{x}, \hat{p}] = i\hbar $$

Expectation Value

The average value of an observable.

$$ \langle \hat{A} \rangle = \langle \psi | \hat{A} | \psi \rangle = \int \psi^* \hat{A} \psi \, d^3r $$

04. Spin & Angular Momentum

Orbital Angular Momentum

$$ \hat{L} = \hat{r} \times \hat{p} $$
$$ [\hat{L}_x, \hat{L}_y] = i\hbar\hat{L}_z $$

Pauli Matrices (Spin-1/2)

Generators of rotation for spin-1/2 particles.

$$ \sigma_x = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \sigma_y = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \sigma_z = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} $$

05. Advanced Systems

Hydrogen Atom Energy Levels

Discrete energy states of the electron in hydrogen.

$$ E_n = -\frac{13.6 \text{ eV}}{n^2} $$

Dirac Equation

Relativistic quantum mechanics for fermions.

$$ (i\gamma^\mu\partial_\mu - m)\psi = 0 $$

Klein-Gordon Equation

Relativistic wave equation for scalar particles.

$$ (\Box + \mu^2)\psi = 0 $$
