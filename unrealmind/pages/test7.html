where: Δx is the uncertainty in position, Δp is the uncertainty in momentum, and ℏ (h-bar) is the reduced Planck constant, approximately equal to 1.054 x 10^-34 Js the core idea ia about the relationship between position and momentum of a particle, we cannot know the position and momentum of a particle at the same time with perfect accuracy.
